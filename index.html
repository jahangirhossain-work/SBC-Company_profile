ENGINEERING &
INDUSTRIAL SERVICES

Saudi Boilers offers Industrial sector clients unique and optimum solutions to maintain equipment's high performance quality and safety levels. We offer services from boiler surveys, inspections, repairs, and revamp, as well as the design, supply, installation, and commissioning of complete boiler systems.

Boilers and auxiliaries are designed, manufactured, installed, and maintained to national and international standards such as ASME BPVC Section I, VIII, VI, BS, European and others. Our Engineering Department has over 30 years of experience in designing, reverse engineering, and evaluating many types of boilers, steam and hot water power plant systems, fuel piping, and pressure vessels.

ASME compliant engineering and reverse engineering.

FABRICATION

SPECIALIZED SERVICES

ADVANCED TECHNICAL SOLUTIONS

Root Cause Analysis

Using influence diagram analysis to decide and determine relative contribution of various root cause influences in operating boilers to prevent recurrence.

Expert Inspection

Proper inspection techniques for all types of boiler components to ensure accurate diagnostics of any failure mode for proper remedies and rehabilitation.

Circulation Analysis

The only team in KSA assessing internal circulation to solve tube overheating. Mitigates reoccurrence of furnace tube failures.

Shutdown Management

Detailed service programs for pressure, auxiliary, and control components to prolong boiler operational life with all safety measures.

Fuel Switch Studies

Technical and feasibility studies for switching fuels to meet environmental and economic constraints (e.g., Liquid to Gas).

Flue Gas Analysis

On-site exhaust gas emission measurements using calibrated, standard, and certified equipment whenever needed.
