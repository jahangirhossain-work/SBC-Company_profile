Comprehensive Solutions

Saudi Boilers offers Industrial sector clients unique and optimum solutions to maintain equipment's high performance quality and safety levels. We offer services from boiler surveys, inspections, repairs, and revamp, as well as the design, supply, installation, and commissioning of complete boiler systems.

Our Engineering Department has over 30 years of experience in designing, reverse engineering, and evaluating many types of boilers, steam and hot water power plant systems, fuel piping, and pressure vessels to ASME standards.

Engineering Consulting

Saudi Boiler provides specialized consulting engineering, supply, installation & rental services based on knowledge-intensive experience. We assist major Saudi industries in providing cost-effective solutions for better, efficient, and reliable continued plant operation.

We believe engineering services translate industrial project concepts into real form through successive applications of intellectual inputs, technology transfer, and local engineering capability promotion.

OUR EXPERT TEAM

Saudi Boiler team is unique among engineering and industrial services providers. The team is the only team in the Middle East that specializes solely in process equipment engineering services.

Most importantly, Saudi Boilers team works with operations and maintenance entities to protect their investments, and to ensure that the contractors and suppliers meet their commitments.
